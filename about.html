About Us

Hello, I'm Pranav Shinde, a Computer Science undergraduate. I'm excited to introduce you to E-Validator, a project that emerged from my passion for web development and data integrity. E-Validator is my solution to the need for a reliable email validation tool. With a focus on both the format and existence of email addresses, the project utilizes advanced algorithms to deliver accurate and instantaneous results. As someone deeply interested in cybersecurity, I believe in the significance of data validation in our digital world. E-Validator is my contribution to ensuring the reliability of online communication. Thank you for choosing E-Validator! Your feedback is invaluable as I continue to enhance and expand the capabilities of this tool. Feel free to reach out with any suggestions or thoughts. Happy validating!
